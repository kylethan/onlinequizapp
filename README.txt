World Quiz is a multiple-choice questions quiz app from different fields or topics from music, culture to science, life knowledge. I want to direct my application in both learning and entertainment. The app is not just a knowledgeably simple quiz, but it is also competitive when the player can compete with other players.
Questions will be randomly assigned to each topic and the player will not have a chance to re-answer the previous question if it has been answered; that will increase the competition for the player and they must make a careful choice before moving on to the next question.
